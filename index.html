About Me

I'm a software developer currently working as a Software Support Engineer at IBM.

I have a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from York University, where I had experience working as a Software Developer Intern at both IBM and CIBC. I enjoy developing apps for all platforms (desktop, mobile, and web). Outside of writing code, I have a couple of hobbies such as playing piano, and playing board games with my friends.

I always had a passion in learning how things worked, specifically in computers. During a high school computer science class, my teacher showed us a video on YouTube called “What Most Schools Don’t Teach”. This video inspired me to learn more about computer software. Writing code felt very satisfying, as it gave me a platform to create whatever I wanted, and showed me immediate results.

My goal is to develop software that many people will use in their daily lives. I want to help create something that I am passionate about, and is useful to people in some way!
